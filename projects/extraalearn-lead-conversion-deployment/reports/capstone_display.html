The Capstone Story

The project translates a business problem into a deployable AI workflow: understand the leads, train the model, and return predictions that support action.

1

Business Problem

ExtraaLearn receives many leads and needs a reliable way to identify which prospects are more likely to become paid customers.

2

Modeling Approach

The notebook compares Random Forest and AdaBoost models, tunes stronger candidates, and selects the best test F1 score for balanced sales usefulness.

3

Deployment Output

A saved model powers a Flask API, while a Streamlit interface supports both single-lead prediction and batch lead scoring.

Data And Evaluation

The dataset contains 4,612 leads, 15 columns, no missing values, and no duplicate records.

Business Recommendations

The model is useful because it connects prediction results to sales and marketing actions.

Prioritize Strong Profiles

Focus first on leads with high profile completion and strong website engagement.

Move Fast On Referrals

Referral leads converted at about 67.74%, making them a high-value outreach segment.

Improve Mobile Onboarding

Use interaction signals to tighten the mobile app journey and reduce lead drop-off.

Refresh Lead Priority

Run batch scoring regularly so sales teams always work from current conversion probabilities.
